    Arena Di Verona

    The Arena Di Verona is a Roman amphitheatre in Piazza Bra in Verona, Italy, built in 30 AD. The area is still being used today for large-scale operas. It is also one of the best ancient preserved structures of its kind.

    Lake Garda

    Lake Garda is located in northan Italy. It is known for its crystal clear water. Lake Garda is Italy's largest lake that is between Venice and Milan. The best time to Visit the lake is during summer. To the south of the lake is a town called Sirmione, near a fortress called Rocca Scaligera with harbor views.
